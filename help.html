Help Center

Frequently asked questions about reporting and claiming items.

How do I report a lost or found item? expand_more

Log in to your dashboard and click "Report Item". Fill out the details including the item's category, where you last saw or found it, and a clear description or photo.

How do I claim an item that belongs to me? expand_more

When you find your item on the Browse page, click "View" and then "This is mine! (Claim)". You will be asked to provide proof of ownership in a short note to the finder.

I claimed an item. What happens next? expand_more

The person who posted the item will review your claim's proof. You can check the status (Pending, Accepted, or Rejected) under the "Claimed" tab in your Dashboard. If they accept your claim, an "Open Chat" button will appear!

Someone claimed my posted item. What should I do? expand_more

Go to the "Received Claims" tab in your Dashboard. You'll see their note and proof of ownership. If it matches, click "Accept". This resolves the item and opens a private chat between you two to arrange a meetup.

Is Camp Find free to use? expand_more

Yes, it is completely free for all verified university and campus community members.
